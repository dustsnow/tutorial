-L[dir]
	include the library dir
-I[dir]
	include the header dir
-l[lib]
	library

How to include gtest
g++ -L/usr/lib/ -I/usr/include/gtest ex.cpp -lgtest -lpthread

How to include fcl
g++ -L/usr/lib/ -I/usr/include/gtest -L/usr/local/include -L/usr/lib/fcl -I/usr/include/fcl ex.cpp -lgtest -lpthread -lfcl
g++ -L/usr/lib/ -I/usr/include/gtest -L/usr/local/include -L/usr/lib/fcl -I/usr/include/fcl ex.cpp -lgtest -lpthread -lfcl
